Walking Paths

True images of the Olympic Park 5 Scenic Courses for Strolling & Jogging

Centering on the Mongchon Fortress, enjoy your strolling or jogging with 5 different courses.

Over a 1.42 km²- wide Olympic Park, enjoy strolling or jogging on Lakeside Lane, Fortress Lane, Memory Lane, Lovers' Lane and Youth Lane.

The Olympic Park is located on a 1.42 km² site, centering on Mongchon Fortress, and is comprised of Lakeside Lane, Fortress Lane, Memory Lane, Lovers' Lane and Youth Laneas the strolling and jogging courses. Walking along the lane, the true images of Olympic Park can be seen with superb vista.

The Lakeside Lane is highly favored by those who work in the nearby areas because they can feel the serenity and peace of mind.

The Fortress Lane, where love of the family can be felt, is favored for its excellent view of the historic relics from the Baekje Dynasty and the wide grass areas, even though the famous "a panting hill" makes it a little difficult.

The Memory Lane which forms two circles around Mongchon Fortress is the best course for quiet thinking and strolling in the late fall.

The Lovers' Lane is suitable for lovers in their tender, leisurely strolls around Lake 88 and the Mongchon Fortress.

Finally, the Youth Lane allows an unobstructed view of the outer ring of Olympic Park and is loved by enthusiastic joggers who love to perspire because it is widely used as part of a marathon or other running events.
