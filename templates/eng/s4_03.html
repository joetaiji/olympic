Hodori train

The Olympic Park's famous Hodori Train is in operation.

If you take the Hodori Train, a specialty of Olympic Park, you can enjoy various facilities and artworks while circulating inside the park.

Hodori Train Reservation

Ticket office and course information

  • running course Please click on the location
  • in front of the museum
  • three-way intersection
  • han-eol Plaza
  • picnic area cycle

Operation section : Peace Plaza ↔ Picnic area

  • Peace Plaza (Departure) → Picnic Area (Round) → Peace Plaza (Arrival)
  • Required time: about 20 minutes / about 5 km

    Hodori trains only run round-trip. (No one way)

Operating days and hours

  • Weekdays (Tuesday-Friday): Operated by online reservation for 20 or more people
  • Sat/Sun/Holiday: Online reservation for one or more people
    • April, May, June, September 11:00 ~ 18:00 - October 11:00 ~ 17:30
    • March, July, August, November 13:00 ~ 17:30

    Departure is possible when more than 15 people board (on-site ticketing is possible for less than 15 people)

  • Internet reservation system (reservation by 18:00 one day before use)
    • Reservation: Hodori Train Reservation System (https://www.ksponco.or.kr/online/train/index.do)
  • It is closed during the winter season (December to February).
  • Events and suspension of operation due to bad weather will be notified separately on the website
