Transport & Parking

Public Transportation is the way to come to Olympic park!

A guide to the parking areas

It’s always exciting to go whether it be with a date, a friend, or family. Unfortunately, the entrances are always busy, full of cars desperately trying to find a place to park. But there is no longer a need to feel anxious on your way to see your favorite artists concert, enjoy the nature, or simply to play a sport! Feel free to enjoy Olympic Park with leisure with your special ones by using public transportation.
