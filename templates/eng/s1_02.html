PVision and Future

The peaceful green place where all life lives together

The Olympic Park is a venue of civic sports, culture, and leisure where the 1988 Seoul Olympics continues to live on.

KSPO (Korea Sports Promotion Foundation) sets up the long-term plan regarding environmental projects of preservation and restoration with all of that, the Olympic Park will be a multifuctional eco-park where all life lives together.

01 Vision-Future The new platform of ecology management setup will include an eco-monitoring system and the adoption of
regional sabbatical terms for nature.
02 Vision-Future With the development of gradual ecological plans focused on biodiversity maintenance and conservation,

the sustainable maintenance system will be conducted in an environment-friendly way. Above all, we will establish the feasible goal of better water quality through various eco-friendly methods over Mongchon Moat and 88 Lake, which have a significant number of ecological elements.
03 Vision-Future The eco-educational programs utilizing the observation deck , info posts

and indoor eco-experience classes will be set up. Once completing the current status research on eco resources, the Olympic Park will publish a flora and fauna map of the park and be formed as the epitome of all eco-parks.
