Sculpture Park

Commemorative place for the Seoul Olympic Games

Feel the glory of the Olympics once again with the marvelous sculptures

The Olympic Sculpture Park is full of the artworks selected from the first and second International Sculpture Symposium & Festival, which were held as a part of the Seoul Olympics Culture & Arts Events.

It was the most important to hold and keep displaying the sculptures in the park even after the Olympic Games finished to make the Olympic Park a symbolic and commemorative place. To achieve this goal, various effective ways such as gathering the related opinions from domestic/international experts and researching on famous foreign sculpture parks have been considered, yet it had a few obstacles while organizing as it was the vast project which has hardly ever done.

However, various supporters including Korean Contemporary Art Festival Committee for International Sculpture Symposium & International Sculpture Invitations put their all efforts, and many international top-notch artists participated in this project. With all the support, the beautiful artistic Olympic Sculpture Park was born and its quality is on the top level around the world.
