Main Route

Minimum walking route from Top 1 (estimated 2 hour / 3.8km)

Main Route- Top Spots, Walking Route
Top Spots Walking Route
Top 1 (World Peace Gate)

Start at the front of the World Peace Gate
Top 3 (Mongchon Musical Fountain)

Passing SOMA Museum, follow the ‘Photo Spot’ signpost
Top 4 (Dialogue)

Go straight passing Mongchon Moat Fountain Mill Wheel and Seoul Olympic Museum of Art, the sculpture is on your left-hand side
Top 5 (Walking Paths)

Follow the ‘Mongchon Fortress’ signpost and ascend the wooden stairs. Go straight for approx. 50m to the left and find the info post before going down the stairs.
Top 6 (One Tree Hill)

Follow the trail to the end, turn right to the toilets and turn left towards the grass which leads you to One Tree Hill
Top 7 (88 Lake)

From the entrance of 88 Lake, follow the first path on the right and find the info post in front of the trash bins
Top 8 (Wild Flower Garden)

Pass the gate 2-3 of the KSPO DOME(Olympic Gymnastics Arena), SK Handball stadium(Fencing stadium) and Woori Art Hall and go over the Baekho Bridge. Find the info post next to wooden stairs in front of the Convention Center
Top 9 (Rose Plaza)

Find the info post in front of the security guard place
Top 2 (The Thumb)

In front of the information center in a meeting plaza.
